About Myself

I am a Ph.D. candidate at College of Information Sciences and Technology, Penn State. I'm working with Dr. Jack Carroll and Dr. Mary Beth Rosson to build and evaluate eduational technolgoies that result in better learning outcome. I'm also working with Dr. Audris Mockus on predictive analytics for customer issues.

My thesis is about understanding software developers' curation activity in social coding site (GitHub), and providing tools to support such activity.
